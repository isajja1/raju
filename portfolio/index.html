About me

Hello! I'm Raju Sajja, a UI/UX designer with a strong background in graphic and logo design. I have graduated from Georgia Tech with a bachelor's degree in Computational Media and a minor in Industrial Design.

Throughout my academic and professional journey, I've been passionate about creating beautiful and effective interfaces for both mobile and web applications. My approach centers on user-centric strategies aimed at achieving real, quantifiable improvements in user engagement and task success.

I have a keen eye for detail and a deep understanding of design principles, which allows me to create visually appealing and functional designs. I am proficient in various design tools such as Adobe Creative Suite, Sketch, and Figma, and I am always eager to learn new technologies and methodologies to enhance my skills.

Vision Statement

"To create intuitive, engaging, and impactful digital experiences that seamlessly connect users with technology, enhancing both functionality and aesthetics through thoughtful and user-centered design."

Mission Statement

"As a UI/UX designer, my mission is to craft meaningful and accessible digital experiences by blending research, creativity, and technology. I strive to solve complex user problems through innovative design solutions, ensuring that every interaction is both intuitive and delightful. By continuously learning and adapting, I aim to drive positive change in the way people engage with digital products."
